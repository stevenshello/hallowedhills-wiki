Housing & Property

Property Tips

Useful advice for managing property without wasting money or creating avoidable headaches.

What it is

This page is the practical advice layer for housing: how to choose a property that helps your character instead of just looking nice on paper.

How to get started

Use it once you already understand the housing basics and are comparing options, planning a move, or deciding whether to expand from apartment life into something bigger.

How it works in practice

Good property choices improve storage, garage access, wardrobe convenience, scene flow, and travel time. Bad ones look fine until you have to live out of them every session.

What to know before you commit

Buy or move with your daily loop in mind. Ask yourself where you work, what you store, who you live with, and whether the place supports the roleplay you actually do.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This page deliberately avoids turning property choice into a meta spreadsheet of secret "best" houses.
