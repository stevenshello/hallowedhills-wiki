Housing & Property

Realtor

How the realtor side of the system supports property acquisition and roleplay.

What it is

The realtor layer is the roleplay and management side of the housing system. It is how properties are created for sale, managed, and moved through the player-facing market rather than simply existing as a static list.

How to get started

Use the realtor flow when you are actually shopping for a place, moving up from apartments, or interacting with a player whose role is to manage property availability.

How it works in practice

Realtors and the `/housing` menu work together with `ps-housing-main` and `ps-realtor-main`. That means listings, property setup, movement into new apartments, and general property handling all tie back into a real system instead of a fake menu wrapper.

What to know before you commit

If your character is serious about property, a realtor is part of the intended loop. That adds roleplay to what would otherwise be a very dry "buy house" transaction.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This page does not publish the staff-grade or creator-side property setup details that are meant for admin or realtor management.
