Housing & Property

Stashes & Storage

The practical storage layer and how homes connect to item security and organization.

What it is

This page explains why housing storage matters so much on Hallowed Hills. A property stash is not just another box; it is one of the main ways players stabilize their item flow.

How to get started

Once you have a property, make sure the stash side of the setup is actually placed and usable. The housing system expects stash and wardrobe placement to be part of making a space functional.

How it works in practice

Property storage supports day-to-day carry management, gear dumping after scenes, staging for jobs, and keeping legal or criminal supply chains organized. It becomes more important the more specialized your character gets.

What to know before you commit

Do not think of storage as optional decoration. If your stash setup is bad, every other loop that generates items or materials feels worse.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

The wiki does not publish backend config lines or admin migration commands for stash setup.
