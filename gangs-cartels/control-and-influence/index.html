Gangs & Cartels

Control & Influence

The competitive pressure layer around territory, presence, events, and server influence.

What it is

This page focuses on Control & Influence inside the Gangs & Cartels section. It is written around the live Hallowed Hills resource stack and meant to explain what the system actually does for a player, not just repeat the page title back to them.

How to get started

This section starts making sense once you are working with a group instead of a solo grind. Read it as a guide to organization, permissions, pressure, and progression rather than just a list of gang-only perks.

How it works in practice

Gang pages usually touch shared permissions, radios, turf pressure, payouts, event control, and who in the organization is allowed to start serious actions. They are less about solo profit and more about collective structure.

What to know before you commit

These systems work best when the group has real internal structure. Recruitment, rank permissions, storage control, mission rights, and conflict discipline matter more than simply flying a shared color.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This guide does not expose hidden gang-only routes, backend permission exploits, or exact contest timings beyond the broad player-facing flow that members should understand.
