Jobs & Careers

Legal Jobs Overview

How the legal economy is split between quick pickup jobs, progression jobs, and custom career-style systems.

What it is

This page breaks legal work into three buckets: simple starter loops, service jobs tied to a real profession, and custom Hallowed Hills career systems that add XP, contracts, or more structure than stock QBCore jobs.

How to get started

If you are brand new, begin with a straightforward loop like bus, garbage, hotdog, taxi, or deliveries. If you already understand the city and want more identity, move into trucking, airline, builder, vineyard, or news.

How it works in practice

The legal side of Hallowed Hills mixes stock QBCore jobs with custom career content. That means some jobs are pure repetition for safe money, while others build in deposits, rank gates, contracts, XP, better vehicles, or bigger routes over time.

What to know before you commit

Do not assume every legal job works the same way. Some require a real job assignment and duty status, some work like open civilian side hustles, and some are custom careers with their own progression.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

Legal-job pages can be specific, but they still avoid staff commands, backend overrides, and any dev-only test hooks.
