Jobs & Careers

Taxi Job

A service-style job built around transport, map knowledge, and short task loops.

What it is

The taxi job is a service route tied to the actual taxi profession. It focuses on passenger pickup and drop-off, with bonuses for not destroying the cab on the way.

How to get started

Make sure your character has the taxi job, go to the taxi yard, clock in if needed, and pull the taxi from the work garage. From there the script begins offering NPC pickup work.

How it works in practice

Pick up a passenger, follow the route to the destination, and keep the cab in decent condition. The script tracks vehicle health and crash count, and cleaner driving is rewarded better than sloppy driving.

What to know before you commit

This is one of the jobs where how you drive matters. Too many crashes or running the cab into the ground can kill the mission flow, so it rewards calm map knowledge more than reckless speed.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

Nothing important is hidden here beyond exact route coordinates and internal mission tables.

Practical Flow

How to actually run it

Step 1

Start at the taxi lot and spawn the taxi.

Step 2

Pick up the marked passenger at the active pickup point.

Step 3

Drive them to the drop-off location without trashing the cab.

Step 4

Keep the taxi healthy to preserve the intact-vehicle bonus.

Step 5

Repeat the next fare or return the taxi when you are done.
