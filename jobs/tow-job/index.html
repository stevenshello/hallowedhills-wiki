Jobs & Careers

Tow Job

A utility-focused job that rewards players who enjoy vehicle handling and service gameplay.

What it is

Tow is a utility-style legal job that sends you to disabled vehicles and pays you for recovering them with a flatbed.

How to get started

Go to Towing HQ, pay the flatbed bail if required, and pull the tow vehicle from the garage. The active job then sends you to a random stranded vehicle.

How it works in practice

Drive to the disabled car, load it onto the flatbed, then return it to the drop-off point. Once the delivery is accepted, the next tow target can roll and the job repeats.

What to know before you commit

Treat this as a vehicle-handling job. The core challenge is recovery and transport, not sprinting between blips on foot.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

The wiki avoids publishing the towspot table, but the actual job structure is simple and fully explained.

Practical Flow

How to actually run it

Step 1

Start at the towing HQ and spawn the flatbed.

Step 2

Drive to the marked broken-down vehicle.

Step 3

Load the vehicle onto the flatbed.

Step 4

Return to the tow drop-off point and complete the recovery.

Step 5

Repeat for the next assignment or return the flatbed when finished.
