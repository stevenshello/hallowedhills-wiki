Legal Work

Jobs & Careers

This section covers the legal work loops across Hallowed Hills, including starter jobs, progression careers, and more custom routes like trucking, airline, and builder work.

Subpages

Drill into the part of this system you actually need

Legal Jobs Overview How the legal economy is split between quick pickup jobs, progression jobs, and custom career-style systems. Bus Job A straightforward city route job that works well for players who want simple legal money with a low barrier to entry. Garbage Job A route-based collection job focused on basic repetition, map movement, and dependable legal payout. Taxi Job A service-style job built around transport, map knowledge, and short task loops. Tow Job A utility-focused job that rewards players who enjoy vehicle handling and service gameplay. Hotdog Job A lightweight entry activity that mixes vending, movement, and casual street-side earning. Builder Job A custom HH construction route with more personality and progression than a basic one-off pickup job. Electric Job A utility repair route where players take a work vehicle, accept assignments, and complete maintenance-style tasks. Deliveries Delivery-style contracts that reward navigation, timing, and keeping a clean route rhythm. Trucking A larger progression career through HH_trucker_tycoon with stronger long-term identity than stock hauler work. Airline A custom aviation career with contracts and progression loops built around flight-based work. Vineyard A slower-paced legal work loop tied to gathering, processing, and business-flavored roleplay. News Job A media-focused route for players who want public-facing reporting and server culture scenes.
