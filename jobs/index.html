Some jobs are available immediately, while others require an application, interview, or staff approval.

How to Apply

Whitelisted positions follow a more structured process than standard jobs.

1. Read the requirements

Check age, activity, behavior, and any department-specific standards before applying.

2. Submit your application

Fill out the application fully and make sure your answers are thoughtful and detailed.

3. Wait for review

Staff or department command will review your application and contact you if selected.

4. Complete interview or training

Some departments may require an interview, academy, ride-along, or probationary phase.

Check the Discord for openings, announcements, and status updates on department applications.

Tips for Getting Hired

  • Stay active and build a positive reputation in the community.
  • Read all server rules before applying for any major role.
  • Use proper grammar and put effort into your application responses.
  • Avoid disciplinary issues if you want the best chance of acceptance.
  • Be patient — command staff are people, not caffeinated robots.
