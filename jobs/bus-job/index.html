Jobs & Careers

Bus Job

A straightforward city route job that works well for players who want simple legal money with a low barrier to entry.

What it is

The bus job is a clean early legal route built around picking up NPC passengers and dropping them at the next stop. It is good for learning the map without a complex setup cost.

How to get started

Go to the bus depot, pull a bus from the garage menu, and stay in the correct work vehicle. The script only progresses while you are actually driving the job bus.

How it works in practice

Once the bus is active, the script sends you to an NPC stop. Pull in, let the passenger board, then follow the new route blip to the drop-off stop. The job pays as the route progresses and immediately rolls into the next pickup/drop cycle.

What to know before you commit

The job expects you to finish the passenger flow cleanly before trying to return the bus. If a passenger is still active, the script pushes you to complete the stop instead of ending the route early.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

No hidden content is being withheld here; this is a public legal loop. The wiki simply avoids low-value dev details like internal event names.

Practical Flow

How to actually run it

Step 1

Go to the bus depot and open the bus vehicle menu.

Step 2

Spawn the bus and get into the driver seat.

Step 3

Follow the route blip to the waiting passenger.

Step 4

Stop close enough for the NPC to board, then follow the new drop-off blip.

Step 5

Pull into the bus stop, let the passenger leave, and continue into the next stop.

Step 6

Only return the bus when you are between passenger cycles and ready to end the route.
