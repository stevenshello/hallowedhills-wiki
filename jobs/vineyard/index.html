Jobs & Careers

Vineyard

A slower-paced legal work loop tied to gathering, processing, and business-flavored roleplay.

What it is

The vineyard job is a slower legal production loop built around grape harvesting and turning that harvest into juice or wine.

How to get started

Go to the vineyard starter area and begin with grape collection. The full value of the job comes from not stopping at raw harvest and instead moving into the processing side.

How it works in practice

Pick grapes, move them into juice or wine processing, and treat the system like an agricultural production chain rather than a single interaction. Wine also has a longer timed process than a quick instant payout.

What to know before you commit

This is a patience job. It works best for players who like consistent legal production rather than constant route driving.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

The wiki leaves out raw coordinates but explains the actual harvest-to-product structure.
