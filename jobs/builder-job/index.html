Builder Job

A custom HH construction route with more personality and progression than a basic one-off pickup job.

What it is

The builder job is one of the more hands-on custom legal routes on the server. It sends you to active construction sites, gives you access to supply hauling, and rotates between job-site tasks like pipes, concrete, walls, and welding.

How to get started

Go to the builder hiring point near city hall, spawn the supply vehicle, and use the truck menu to grab the supplies needed for the site tasks. The script is explicit that you should get supplies first and then work the marked site.

How it works in practice

Once the site is active, walk up and work the site with the required supply type. The script picks from the available tasks you are carrying and pays for each completed action after the progress bar finishes.

What to know before you commit

Builder is most fun if you treat it like a job site instead of just a money clicker. Bring the right supplies, follow the GPS to the current site, and work through the construction flavor the script is trying to create.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This page does not publish the exact site coordinates, but it does explain the actual supply-and-task loop.

Practical Flow

How to actually run it

Step 1

Take the builder job and go to the hiring/start point.

Step 2

Spawn the supply vehicle and use its menu to take pipes, concrete, wall parts, or welding kits.

Step 3

Follow the GPS to the active construction site.

Step 4

Press the site interaction prompt and complete the available task you have supplies for.

Step 5

Finish the progress bar, collect the payout, and continue with the next site task.
