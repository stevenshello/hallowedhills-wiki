Trucking

A larger progression career through HH_trucker_tycoon with stronger long-term identity than stock hauler work.

What it is

Trucking is a real progression career on Hallowed Hills rather than just a simple haul script. It gives you levels, XP, deposits, distance-based pay, damage penalties, and tier unlocks that gradually move you from local box work into long-haul rigs.

How to get started

Head to LS Docks, talk to the dock worker, and start with the local tier vehicles if your level is fresh. The system takes a deposit and uses XP to unlock better trucks and more serious route tiers over time.

How it works in practice

Take a job, drive the assigned cargo run, complete the delivery, and let the system calculate payout from base pay, distance, level bonus, and truck damage. Finishing cleanly pushes both money and XP, which is what unlocks the stronger tiers.

What to know before you commit

This is the kind of legal career that pays back patience. It feels better the longer you stay in it because the level curve opens better vehicles and larger routes instead of leaving you on the same starter task forever.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

The wiki avoids the exact stop table but covers the progression logic and job rhythm directly.

Practical Flow

How to actually run it

Step 1

Go to LS Docks and speak to the dock worker.

Step 2

Start with a local-tier truck if you are new to the job.

Step 3

Pay the required deposit and take the active route.

Step 4

Drive the delivery carefully, because truck damage directly cuts into payout and XP.

Step 5

Turn the job in, collect pay and XP, and keep leveling until regional and long-haul unlock.
