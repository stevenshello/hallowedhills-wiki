Hotdog Job

A lightweight entry activity that mixes vending, movement, and casual street-side earning.

What it is

The hotdog job is a mobile street-selling side hustle built around taking out a stand, preparing stock, and selling to passing NPCs. It also has a small reputation ladder that improves your stock capacity and sale range.

How to get started

Go to the hotdog start point, pay the stand deposit, and take out the stand. Once it is placed, prepare stock before you try to toggle sales, because you cannot sell what you have not cooked.

How it works in practice

You prepare common, rare, or exotic hotdogs, then toggle selling and let nearby NPCs become potential customers. Sales adjust your stock and reputation, and higher hotdog rep opens better stock ceilings and price bands.

What to know before you commit

This job is less about one big payout and more about rhythm: place stand, build stock, turn sales on, sell to passing people, then pack up cleanly so you get the deposit back.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

The only things kept out here are internal prices-by-level math and low-value event plumbing. The real player loop is all above ground.

Practical Flow

How to actually run it

Step 1

Go to the hotdog stand starter and take out the stand with the required deposit.

Step 2

Use the stand to prepare stock before trying to sell.

Step 3

Turn selling on so nearby pedestrians can become customers.

Step 4

Sell from your current stock and watch your hotdog reputation increase over time.

Step 5

Pack the stand back up and end cleanly so the deposit is returned.
