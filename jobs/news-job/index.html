News Job

A media-focused route for players who want public-facing reporting and server culture scenes.

What it is

The news job is less about farming cash and more about giving players a real media identity inside the city. It provides the newsroom locations, vehicles, helicopter access, and the camera/mic equipment needed for reporter scenes.

How to get started

Use the Weazel News HQ as your anchor point, access the assigned vehicles or helicopter if your grade allows it, and pull the recording gear from the job vehicle setup.

How it works in practice

The loop is driven by roleplay: show up to scenes, report on events, interview people, document city activity, and use the news equipment as part of live story coverage.

What to know before you commit

This job shines when players actively build scenes with it. The script gives you the gear and transport, but the value comes from using it to create public-facing RP.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

There is nothing secret here beyond internal config details; this page is mainly about what the job supports.
