Jobs & Careers

Deliveries

Delivery-style contracts that reward navigation, timing, and keeping a clean route rhythm.

What it is

Deliveries is a package-run legal job that feels more like a full shift than a one-click side activity. It uses a dispatcher NPC, a van deposit, uniform change, and repeated package handoffs.

How to get started

Talk to Michael to begin the shift, change into the delivery outfit, and take out the van. The script charges a van deposit, so ending properly matters if you want the full value back.

How it works in practice

Pick up packages from the van, follow the route to each drop, hand the package to the waiting NPC, then move to the next stop. The timer matters, and late deliveries reduce the payout rather than failing the whole route outright.

What to know before you commit

This job rewards route discipline more than anything else. Keep the van safe, keep the packages moving, and do not forget the return-to-Michael step at the end or you leave money on the table.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

The wiki explains the working loop, but not the exact delivery table or any backend framework setup.

Practical Flow

How to actually run it

Step 1

Talk to Michael to start the delivery shift.

Step 2

Change into the work outfit and spawn the van.

Step 3

Take a package from the van.

Step 4

Drive to the active delivery point and hand the package to the waiting NPC.

Step 5

Repeat until all packages are delivered.

Step 6

Return to Michael with the van to end the shift and recover the deposit.
