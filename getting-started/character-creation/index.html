Guide: Getting Started

Character Creation

How the multicharacter flow, spawn choice, city hall, and clothing setup shape the first session.

What it is

This page explains what actually happens between picking a character slot and becoming a live citizen in Hallowed Hills. It covers the preview scene, clothing load, gender-based freemode setup, and how the spawn flow hands you into the city.

How to get started

Use the character selector to preview or create your character, then complete the normal creation path instead of rushing through appearance and first location choices. That first setup shapes how the rest of your first session feels.

How it works in practice

The multicharacter system stages you in a hidden interior, previews your ped, and loads clothing if the character already exists. From there, the spawn system decides whether you go to last location, a house, an apartment, or a normal city spawn. Fresh characters tie directly into apartment and clothing setup.

What to know before you commit

Character creation is not just cosmetic. Your first spawn choice, clothing state, apartment start, and early city-hall interactions all branch from this step.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This guide does not publish developer-only spawn editing, hidden rebel spawn talent checks, or backend metadata edits.
