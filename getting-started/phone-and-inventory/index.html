Guide: Getting Started

Phone & Inventory

The core utility systems players use every session, from their phone and radial menus to storage and item handling.

What it is

This page explains the two systems you will touch every session: your phone and your inventory. Between them, they handle communication, utility access, storage decisions, and a huge amount of moment-to-moment gameplay.

How to get started

Get comfortable with these two systems before you attempt a complicated job or criminal route. A surprising amount of "I am stuck" problems in RP servers come from not knowing where your items, app flow, or interaction menus live.

How it works in practice

The phone is your communications and utility hub, while inventory drives item handling, transfers, storage, and job readiness. Hallowed Hills also layers in radial and HUD systems that make these tools part of almost every daily routine.

What to know before you commit

If you cannot find an app, a contact, an item, or a carry/storage action, the problem is usually here first. Mastering these systems makes every other page on the wiki easier.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

The wiki explains player-facing usage, not staff-only tools, exploit chains, or hidden app/debug hooks.
