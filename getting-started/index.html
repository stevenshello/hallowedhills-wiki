Before You Join

Before jumping into Hallowed Hills, make sure you:

  • Join the official Discord server
  • Read the Server Rules
  • Review the Roleplay Expectations
  • Make sure your game and any required content are installed
  • Complete any required verification or whitelist steps

Your First Time In-Game

  1. Create a realistic character
  2. Stay in character unless directed otherwise by staff
  3. Read any onboarding or tutorial information provided in-game
  4. Follow staff instructions during setup
  5. Ask for help in the proper channels if you are confused

Core Expectations

Every player is expected to understand the basics of roleplay:

  • Stay in character during active roleplay
  • Value your life and act realistically
  • Do not random kill, troll, or intentionally ruin scenes
  • Do not use out-of-character information in character
  • Treat staff and other community members with respect
