Guide: Getting Started

Early Progression

How to turn your first few sessions into a stable character path through housing, jobs, vehicles, and social circles.

What it is

This page connects the first few sessions together: home base, first cash flow, transport, storage, and deciding whether your character is leaning into legal work, business, gathering, emergency services, or the criminal side of the city.

How to get started

Once you can reliably spawn, navigate, and make money, start building permanence. That usually means securing transport, understanding property, and figuring out which section of the wiki your character will keep coming back to.

How it works in practice

Early progression is less about one single script and more about how multiple systems stack: housing, money, keys, garages, phone use, reputation, and the first social groups or departments you touch.

What to know before you commit

This is where a character stops feeling temporary. If you spend your first few sessions learning one route deeply and linking it to housing and storage, the rest of the server opens up much more cleanly.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This page is about intended progression and pacing, not shortcutting talent, credit, or illegal systems before your character is ready.
