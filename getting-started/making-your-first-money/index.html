Guide: Getting Started

Making Your First Money

Safe legal options for early cash flow before a player moves into businesses, property, or higher-risk content.

What it is

This page is about realistic early cash flow, not jackpot chasing. It points players toward the legal systems that reliably move a character from broke starter status into stable early progression.

How to get started

Use a simple legal loop first: bus, garbage, taxi, hotdog, deliveries, or another starter-friendly route. The goal is not the coolest job in the city on day one; it is a repeatable income stream that teaches the map and keeps your character solvent.

How it works in practice

The early economy on Hallowed Hills expects you to learn simple task loops, deposits, vehicle returns, and payout collection. Once those basics click, it becomes much easier to branch into property, custom careers, gathering, or higher-risk content.

What to know before you commit

The best first-money system is the one you will actually repeat without getting lost or burning out. Consistent bank balance beats one flashy payout followed by being broke again.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This guide focuses on intended legal early-game routes, not hidden criminal shortcuts or spoiler-heavy exploit paths.
