Custom Hallowed Hills Systems

Yachts

The higher-end lifestyle or event content around custom yacht systems.

What it is

Yachts are part of the higher-end custom content layer and help give wealthy or event-focused characters something more distinctive than a normal ground-based routine.

How to get started

Treat yacht content as lifestyle or event content, not as a beginner progression target. It makes more sense once your character already has money, transport, and a reason to step into luxury-facing RP.

How it works in practice

The yacht layer works as premium-flavored content and scene space. It adds more identity to the top end of the city and gives players a different backdrop for higher-status play.

What to know before you commit

This content is best when used as roleplay value, not just mechanical value.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

The wiki avoids exact spawn or interaction locations for premium event-style systems.
