Cayo Perico

The island-flavored content layer and how it fits into the broader city experience.

What it is

Cayo Perico is the island-side content layer that expands the feeling of the server beyond the normal city map. It gives Hallowed Hills a more adventurous and event-capable edge.

How to get started

Use this once your character is already comfortable with the city and ready for broader travel or special-location content.

How it works in practice

Cayo is less about routine city errands and more about giving the server another stage for events, criminal flavor, and high-end exploration-style content.

What to know before you commit

Special-location content works best when you treat it as its own atmosphere, not just as another place to sprint for a payout.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

The exact travel or interaction details stay off the wiki so the island keeps some discovery value.
