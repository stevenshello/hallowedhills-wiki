Custom Hallowed Hills Systems

HH Market

The custom market and trade flavor that feeds both legal and semi-legal character progression.

What it is

HH Market is part of the server's custom flavor around buying, selling, and value movement. It helps make legal and semi-legal progress feel tied to a city economy rather than disconnected menu payouts.

How to get started

This system matters once your character is already moving enough goods, money, or business activity to care about market behavior and value shifts.

How it works in practice

The market layer overlaps with shops, item value, business play, and the broader feeling of a living economy. It is less about one isolated script and more about how the server frames trade and progression value.

What to know before you commit

Think of this page as "how the city values things," not just "where to click to sell something."

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This page does not publish behind-the-scenes balancing knobs or hidden market routes.
