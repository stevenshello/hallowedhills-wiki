Custom Hallowed Hills Systems

Talent Tree

The HH perk and progression layer that influences how players specialize over time.

What it is

The talent tree is a major long-term progression layer on Hallowed Hills. It lets players specialize across civilian, police, medical, global, and illegal paths instead of staying flat forever.

How to get started

Treat the talent tree as a long-term investment system, not something you finish in a weekend. It becomes more meaningful as you settle into the activities your character actually repeats.

How it works in practice

XP feeds level thresholds, levels create perk-point access, and perk trees change how gathering, processing, criminal work, emergency roles, and other loops feel over time. That means the tree quietly shapes many parts of the server even when you are not staring at the UI.

What to know before you commit

This system rewards consistency. The more clearly your character leans into a lane, the better the talent tree feels.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

The wiki explains the purpose of the perk system, not every internal value, unlock requirement, or hidden edge case.
