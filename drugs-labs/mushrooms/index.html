Drugs & Labs

Mushrooms

A separate psychedelic branch that gives criminal characters more variety than a single product pipeline.

What it is

This page focuses on Mushrooms inside the Drugs & Labs section. It is written around the live Hallowed Hills resource stack and meant to explain what the system actually does for a player, not just repeat the page title back to them.

How to get started

Move into this layer after you have basic crime knowledge, some income, and the patience to learn a longer production chain. Expect preparation, supply handling, and more pressure than simple street crime.

How it works in practice

Drug systems usually chain together sourcing, handling, processing, movement, and sale pressure. The important thing is understanding the ladder from beginner-friendly product lines into bigger lab or mission-backed operations.

What to know before you commit

Production loops usually cost more time and setup than people expect. You will get more out of this layer if you already have transport, safe storage, and a plan for what happens after the product exists.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This guide does not publish lab coordinates, exact minigame answers, ingredient puzzle strings, or backend shortcuts that would turn production into a copy-paste spoiler sheet.
