Businesses & Economy

Economy Overview

The broad shape of legal money, account flow, cash management, and why different loops matter at different stages.

What it is

This page explains the broader money shape of Hallowed Hills: how characters move from starter wages into stable banking, owned assets, credit, and larger business-facing decisions.

How to get started

Use it after you already understand first-money loops. This is the page for deciding how to hold onto money, where to reinvest it, and which systems actually build long-term stability instead of just short bursts of cash.

How it works in practice

The economy layer connects bank funds, cash flow, storage, vehicles, housing, business ownership, credit products, and even some gray-market movement. The point is not just "make money," but "make money in a way your character can keep."

What to know before you commit

A strong economy page is really a progression page. Good financial habits open better vehicles, better property, easier job flexibility, and safer experimentation.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This page avoids exploit-sensitive valuation info and backend economic levers that are not useful to ordinary players.
