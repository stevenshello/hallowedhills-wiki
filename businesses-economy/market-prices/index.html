Businesses & Economy

Market Prices

A market-facing overview of pricing, trade value, and how the economy rewards specialization.

What it is

This page focuses on Market Prices inside the Businesses & Economy section. It is written around the live Hallowed Hills resource stack and meant to explain what the system actually does for a player, not just repeat the page title back to them.

How to get started

Approach this page once you already understand the first-money layer and want to move into longer-term cash flow, property, credit, or business ownership. These systems matter more once your character can sustain recurring costs.

How it works in practice

This layer affects recurring bills, account management, purchasing power, ownership, and how safely a character can branch into more expensive loops. It is where short-term money starts turning into actual stability or leverage.

What to know before you commit

This side of the server punishes impatience. Loans, business ownership, and storage-heavy systems are useful once you can support them, but they will feel bad if you rush them before your character has a steady money loop.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This guide does not publish staff-only tools, exploit-sensitive backend logic, answer strings for gated minigames, or hidden coordinates that are supposed to be discovered through play.
