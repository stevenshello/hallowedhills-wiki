Crime

This page covers criminal roleplay expectations, offense categories, warrants, evidence, arrests, and sentencing guidelines. If you want chaos, cool — just make it good RP and not brain-dead nonsense.

Crime RP Basics

  • Value your life and the scene.
  • Keep escalation believable.
  • No random crime spam for no reason.
  • Accept consequences when caught.

Staff Note

Staff may intervene in criminal scenes that become unrealistic, exploitative, low-effort, or clearly done to farm clips instead of roleplay.

General Expectations

Criminal roleplay should create tension, stories, and consequences — not just constant shootouts or speedrun felony simulator garbage.

  • Criminal actions must have believable motive and reasonable escalation.
  • Players committing crimes are expected to roleplay fear, pressure, and consequences.
  • Using mechanics, loopholes, or server systems in bad faith is not valid criminal RP.
  • Repeat violent crime with no meaningful story may be limited by staff.
  • Large-scale crime may require additional faction approval or staff oversight.

Offense Categories

Infractions / Minor Offenses

  • Trespassing
  • Disorderly conduct
  • Vandalism
  • Petty theft
  • Resisting without violence

Misdemeanor-Level Crime

  • Simple assault
  • Possession of illegal items
  • Evading police (low-level)
  • Breaking and entering
  • Criminal threats

Felony-Level Crime

  • Armed robbery
  • Kidnapping
  • Grand theft auto
  • Drug trafficking
  • Weapons trafficking

Violent / Major Crime

  • Attempted murder
  • Murder / manslaughter
  • Aggravated assault with a deadly weapon
  • Hostage taking
  • Organized criminal activity

Penalties

Penalties should match the severity of the crime, prior record, evidence, and the total scope of the incident. Use the chart below as a framework and adjust it to your server’s actual laws.

Category Typical Fine Typical Jail Time Notes
Minor Offense $250 – $1,000 0 – 10 months Often resolved with citation or short booking.
Misdemeanor $1,000 – $5,000 10 – 30 months May include probation or seized property.
Felony $5,000 – $20,000 30 – 80 months Enhanced by prior history or use of weapons.
Violent / Major $20,000+ 80+ months May involve long-term investigations and warrant stacking.

Replace these numbers with your final economy-balanced charges before publishing.

Warrants & Investigations

  • Search warrants require probable cause and appropriate approval.
  • Arrest warrants may be issued from officer reports, witness statements, or evidence chains.
  • Ongoing investigations should rely on actual RP and collected evidence — not guesswork.
  • Police should document evidence clearly before major raid or arrest actions.

Evidence

  • Photos, bodycam, CCTV, and witness statements may support charges.
  • Physical evidence must be roleplayed appropriately when applicable.
  • Players should not meta or powergame around hidden evidence.
  • Destroyed, missing, or weak evidence may reduce or prevent charges.

Justice Process

  1. Detainment: Suspect is stopped, searched, or secured based on reasonable suspicion or probable cause.
  2. Arrest: Charges are explained and the suspect is processed according to the scene.
  3. Evidence Review: Officers confirm applicable charges and supporting evidence.
  4. Sentencing: Jail time, fines, seizure, or release is determined according to server law.
  5. Court RP: Major cases may proceed to judge-led hearings, warrant review, or appeals.

What Not To Do

  • Do not commit nonstop violent crime with zero story or consequence.
  • Do not use server restarts, safe zones, or mechanics to dodge RP.
  • Do not bait police purely for shootouts.
  • Do not treat jail like a joke if the scene was serious.
