Truck Robbery

A moving-target crime loop where setup and route control matter more than brute speed.

What it is

This page explains the general player-facing purpose of Truck Robbery inside Hallowed Hills. It focuses on the role the system plays in progression, scene building, and day-to-day play without exposing hidden answers, illegal coordinates, or sensitive backend details.

How to get started

The safest way to approach this system is to enter through the normal progression attached to it: build the right contacts, complete the obvious starter steps, and use the broader category page as your first checkpoint before you commit resources or crew members.

What to expect

Players should expect this system to connect to several other loops on the server, whether that means money, item flow, law pressure, social reputation, or access to deeper custom HH content.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This guide does not publish hidden locations, exact answer strings, staff-only controls, exploit-sensitive logic, or spoiler-style shortcuts that would flatten the intended progression.
