Gathering, Fishing & Hunting

Hunting

The hunting layer and what players should expect from that style of outdoor progression.

What it is

This page focuses on Hunting inside the Gathering, Fishing & Hunting section. It is written around the live Hallowed Hills resource stack and meant to explain what the system actually does for a player, not just repeat the page title back to them.

How to get started

These pages are a good early or mid-game path if you want legal, self-directed progress. They explain how to turn time in the world into materials, sale goods, and later crafting value.

How it works in practice

This resource layer feeds sale goods, crafting inputs, and self-sufficient progression. It is one of the cleanest ways for a new or quiet character to build value without immediately stepping into high-pressure scenes.

What to know before you commit

The payoff here is usually steadier than flashier systems, but it scales better when you know which materials you want and where they feed into crafting, sale, or business loops.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This guide does not publish staff-only tools, exploit-sensitive backend logic, answer strings for gated minigames, or hidden coordinates that are supposed to be discovered through play.
