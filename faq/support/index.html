FAQ & Support

Support

Where to go when the wiki is not enough and a player genuinely needs staff help.

What it is

This page focuses on Support inside the FAQ & Support section. It is written around the live Hallowed Hills resource stack and meant to explain what the system actually does for a player, not just repeat the page title back to them.

How to get started

Use this page when a player is stuck, unsure what key or menu they need, or needs a straight answer before jumping into the deeper section pages.

How it works in practice

These pages are written to solve the common "what am I missing?" moments: menus not opening, controls being forgotten, scenes feeling bugged, or a player not knowing which system page they actually need next.

What to know before you commit

If the answer here still does not solve it, the next move is usually the broader section page or support process, not random experimentation that could cost you gear, money, or scene momentum.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This guide does not publish staff-only tools, exploit-sensitive backend logic, answer strings for gated minigames, or hidden coordinates that are supposed to be discovered through play.
