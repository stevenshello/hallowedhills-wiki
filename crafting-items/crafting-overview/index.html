Crafting & Items

Crafting Overview

The broad crafting layer, where it fits in progression, and how it supports both legal and illegal play.

What it is

This page focuses on Crafting Overview inside the Crafting & Items section. It is written around the live Hallowed Hills resource stack and meant to explain what the system actually does for a player, not just repeat the page title back to them.

How to get started

Use this page once gathering, shopping, or mission rewards start feeding into benches, components, and useful equipment. It is meant to explain what the item layer does for progression, not dump every recipe on one screen.

How it works in practice

This section sits between raw materials and finished capability. Expect benches, utility items, carry capacity, reload systems, display gear, and progression items to overlap with both legal and illegal play.

What to know before you commit

The item layer is easiest to understand if you stop thinking in single recipes and start thinking in supply chains. Materials, rare pieces, storage, and utility gear usually matter together.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This guide does not publish staff-only tools, exploit-sensitive backend logic, answer strings for gated minigames, or hidden coordinates that are supposed to be discovered through play.
