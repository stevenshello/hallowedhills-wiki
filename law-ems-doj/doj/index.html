Police, EMS & DOJ

DOJ

The legal paperwork and court-flavored side of the justice layer.

What it is

This page focuses on DOJ inside the Police, EMS & DOJ section. It is written around the live Hallowed Hills resource stack and meant to explain what the system actually does for a player, not just repeat the page title back to them.

How to get started

Use this page if you are joining a whitelisted emergency or justice role, or if you need to understand how those departments will approach your scenes. Most of these systems are structured roleplay tools rather than simple payouts.

How it works in practice

These systems revolve around scene response, evidence, reports, treatment, paperwork, and coordination between departments. Expect procedure and role discipline to matter more here than raw cash output.

What to know before you commit

Most of this content expects department standards and communication. You are stepping into a framework with procedure, chain-of-command, and scene expectations, not just a new paycheck.

What this wiki leaves out on purpose

This guide does not publish staff-only tools, exploit-sensitive backend logic, answer strings for gated minigames, or hidden coordinates that are supposed to be discovered through play.
